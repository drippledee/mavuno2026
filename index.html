🎟️ What is the MAVUNO Raffle?

The MAVUNO Raffle is an exciting initiative to support our annual student fundraiser. 100% of the proceeds from your ticket purchases go directly toward funding community impact projects, supporting student welfare, and driving local outreach initiatives.

How It Works:

  • 1. Choose Your Edition: Pick the ticket tier that matches your status (Student, Alumni, or Supporter).
  • 2. Secure via M-Pesa: Click 'Buy Ticket' and complete your payment to our secure Till Number.
  • 3. Get Verified: Keep your transaction code. Once verified, your official ticket number is locked into the draw.
  • 4. The Grand Draw: Winners will be picked randomly during our main event on November 7, 2026!
